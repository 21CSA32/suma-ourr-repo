##**LaunchanRDSDatabaseInstance**
###**Step1:GotoAWSRDSConsole**
1.Signinto**AWSManagementConsole**.
2.Navigateto**RDS**‚ÜíClick**Createdatabase**.

###**Step2:ChooseDatabaseEngine**
-Select**EngineType**:

-**Version**:Choosethelatestavailable.

###**Step3:ConfigureDatabaseSettings**

-**MasterUsername**:`admin`
-**MasterPassword**:`your-strong-password`

###**Step4:SelectInstanceClass**
-**DBInstanceClass**:
-**FreeTier**:`db.t3.micro`
-**Production**:`db.t3.medium`orhigher

###**Step5:StorageSettings**
-**AllocatedStorage**:`20GB`(ormoreifneeded)
-**StorageAutoScaling**:‚úÖEnabled(optional)

-**DatabaseAuthentication**:Choose**PasswordAuthentication**.

###**Step7:AdditionalConfiguration**
-**InitialDatabaseName**:`mydatabase`
-**BackupRetention**:7days(default)
-**PerformanceInsights**:Enable(Optional)

###**Step8:CreateDatabase**
Click**CreateDatabase**andwaitfortheRDSinstancetobeavailable.

##**üîπ2     ConfigureSecurityGroupforRDS**
###**Step1:GettheRDSSecurityGroup**
1.Goto**EC2Dashboard**‚Üí**SecurityGroups**.
2.Findthesecuritygroupassociatedwithyour**RDSinstance**.

###**Step2:ModifyInboundRules**
1.Clickon**Editinboundrules**.
2.Addanewrule:
-**Type**:MySQL/Aurora
-**Protocol**:TCP
-**PortRange**:`3306`
-**Source**:**EC2SecurityGroup**(Not0.0.0.0/0forsecurity)
3.Click**SaveRules**.

##**üîπ5Ô∏è‚É£ConnectEC2toRDS**
###**Step1:GetRDSEndpoint**
1.Goto**RDSDashboard**‚ÜíClickonyour**RDSinstance**.
2.Find**Endpoint**under**Connectivity&security**.
3.Copythe**Endpoint**(e.g.,`mydb-instance.xxxxxxxxxx.us-east-1.rds.amazonaws.com`).

